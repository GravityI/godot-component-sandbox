Shockwave Shader took from https://youtu.be/VKciJWqVoXQ
Example made based on https://github.com/bbaki/godot-fullscreen-shockwave-example

Setup:
	- Add "FXCanvas.tscn" to your scene.
	- Call the trigger_shockwave method from FXCanvas/ShockwaveRect passing the center position of the shockwave as a parameter.
